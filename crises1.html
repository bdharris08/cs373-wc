History

Bath Salts are legal designer drugs sold as "bath salts" where users snort the bath salts for a high similar to amphetamines. The chemicals in bath salts have been around for almost a century but in the last decade bath salts have emerged in mainstream America as a legal way to get "high." The drug first came to the attention of authorities in 2010 after reports from U.S. poison centers.
