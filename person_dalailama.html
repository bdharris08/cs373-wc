Biography

The 14th Dalai Lama, Jetsun Jamphel Ngawang Lobsang Yeshe Tenzin Gyatso (Tenzin Gyatso for short), was born Lhamo Dondrub in Takster, China. He is the spiritual leader of the Gelugpa lineage of Tibetan Buddhists, and after fleeing to India in the 1959 Tibetan Uprising against China, he established the Tibetan government in exile. Since then, he has traveled the world teaching Tibetan Buddhism and advocating for the welfare of Tibetans.
